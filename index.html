Lifestyle

Purpose

Throughout our lives there comes a time when we ask ourselves what else can I do to grow as a person and you have that feeling of emptiness because you can't find a direction, has it happened to you or is it happening to you?

Audience

My website will focus on the attention of all those who want to improve their lifestyle, who want to find a better approach to life, it will be about topics related to personal development, in this website you can register and you will have the opportunity to share thoughts and feelings anonymously, you can ask questions and also reflect on questions that you have been asked.

Home page

This space arises from the need to find practical tools that help us to materialize concepts such as gratitude, trust in life, connection with the mystery and many other topics. From Vivir Agradecidos we feel the desire to offer a space where we can share in community our reflections on these topics, and that we can apply them to daily life in a very concrete way, in order to be fuller, healthier and more grateful people.
